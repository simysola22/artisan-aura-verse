PHASE 2 — NO PERMANENT SHARED ACCESS KEYS
==================================================

Do not create one permanent shared “Admin Key.”

The system must use individually attributable authenticated identities.

If emergency or elevated access codes are eventually needed, they must be:

- Individually issued
- Short-lived or rotatable
- Revocable
- Hashed at rest where appropriate
- Never displayed again after creation
- Never stored in plaintext
- Never exposed to the frontend unnecessarily
- Fully audited
- Associated with a specific staff member
- Subject to expiry and revocation
- Protected against brute force
- Protected against replay where applicable

However, do not implement an emergency credential system unless the audit proves that it is necessary.

Prefer Clerk identities + PMP roles + permissions as the primary access model.
